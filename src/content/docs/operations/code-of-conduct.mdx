## Our Commitment

Dear Asian Youth is committed to creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all community members, volunteers, and staff. We embrace diversity and believe that everyone should be able to participate fully in our mission.

## Expected Behavior

All participants in our community are expected to:

- **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with dignity and respect
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Be collaborative** - Work together constructively
- **Be accountable** - Take responsibility for your actions
- **Be supportive** - Help create an environment where everyone can thrive

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable:

- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neurotype, physical appearance, body size, age, race, or religion
- Threats of violence, incitement of violence, or encouraging self-harm
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Harassing photography or recording
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Disruptive trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to leadership or through our designated reporting channels.

## Enforcement

Participants asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately. We may take any action we deem appropriate, including removal from the community.

---

*This Code of Conduct applies to all Dear Asian Youth spaces, both online and offline.*
