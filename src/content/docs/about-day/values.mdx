---
title: Our Values
description: The principles that guide our work and our community.
---

Our values are the foundation of our organization and guide every decision we make.

- **Empowerment**: We believe in providing youth with the tools, resources, and platform to become leaders and changemakers.
- **Community**: We foster a safe, inclusive, and supportive global community where Asian youth can connect, share, and grow.
- **Advocacy**: We stand up against injustice and advocate for the issues that matter to Asian communities worldwide.
- **Education**: We are committed to learning and educating others about Asian history, culture, and current events.
- **Authenticity**: We celebrate the diversity of Asian identities and encourage everyone to bring their authentic selves to our community.
- **Collaboration**: We work with other organizations and individuals to amplify our impact and achieve common goals.
