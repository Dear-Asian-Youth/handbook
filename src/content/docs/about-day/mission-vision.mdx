## Our Mission

To empower and uplift Asian youth through advocacy, education, and community building, ensuring their voices are heard and their stories are told.

## Our Vision

We envision a world where Asian youth are seen, heard, and empowered to create the change they want to see in the world. We strive for a future of equity, representation, and justice for all.
