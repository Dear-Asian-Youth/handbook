The Finance & Fundraising team raises funds for DAY and meaningful causes, manages grants, analyzes trends to inform strategy, and administers sound fiscal policies and plans. The team oversees financial activities and reports on revenue to support DAY's mission.

### What we do

- Fundraise for DAY and aligned causes
- Write grants and manage applications
- Analyze financial and operational data to guide strategy
- Develop and administer fiscal policies and plans
- Oversee financial activities and report on revenue
