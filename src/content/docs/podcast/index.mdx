Dear Asian Generation is a podcast uplifting, highlighting, and supporting Asian girls. With an authentic, relatable voice, we turn storytelling into collective conversations to raise awareness of the issues we face and help lead change.

### What we do

- Produce and publish episodes featuring community voices and guests
- Amplify stories and perspectives of Asian girls
- Facilitate conversations on issues impacting our community
- Build awareness and inspire action through audio storytelling
